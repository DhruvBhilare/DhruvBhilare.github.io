About Me

Hello! I am Dhruv Bhilare, a freshman majoring in Computer Engineering at the University of Cincinnati. Born and raised in India, I moved to the United States to further my education and pursue my passion for robotics and innovation. My expertise lies in designing circuits, building robots, and developing practical engineering solutions through hands-on experimentation.

With a strong foundation in both hardware and software, I enjoy working on projects that blend electronics, programming, and mechanical design. My goal is to create innovative and efficient technologies that solve real-world problems.

Beyond engineering, I enjoy playing basketball, working out, and listening to music. These hobbies keep me motivated, help me maintain a balanced lifestyle, and inspire creativity in my projects.


This website serves as a portfolio of my work, showcasing my projects, ideas, and technical expertise. I am always eager to learn, collaborate, and push the boundaries of what’s possible in robotics and technology.
