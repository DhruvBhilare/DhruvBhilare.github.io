The Idea

VisionMate is my latest creation: (As of 2|28|25)

An AI-powered, wearable device designed to enhance accessibility for the visually impaired. The inspiration came from a real-life encounter at the Lindner Center of Business, where I saw firsthand the challenges faced by a blind individual. It made me reflect on how much I take my own 20/20 vision for granted—simple tasks like reading a menu, recognizing faces, or distinguishing between a $1 and a $20 bill are daily struggles for over 300 million visually impaired people worldwide.

Existing solutions are often slow, expensive, and outdated. With VisionMate, I set out to create a lightweight, intuitive, and affordable solution that enables users to experience their surroundings in real time.

Note: This project was developed during a hackathon with valuable contributions from my teammates:

    • How It Works

    VisionMate uses Google Gemini 1.5 Flash to analyze visual input from a camera mounted on a pair of sunglasses. The AI-generated descriptions are then converted into speech using Google Text-to-Speech, which is delivered through bone-conducting speakers, allowing users to stay aware of their environment while receiving information. But VisionMate does more than just describe surroundings:

  • Currency Recognition – Instantly differentiates between various denominations.
  • Text Translation – Reads and translates text into the user’s preferred language.
  • Art Interpretation – Provides abstract descriptions of artworks for cultural enrichment.
  • Real-Time Object & Scene Recognition – Helps users understand their immediate environment.

    • What's Next

    VisionMate is currently in its first prototype phase, but I have ambitious plans for future development:

  • Reducing device size for improved portability and comfort.
  • Enhancing AI accuracy for better object recognition and more natural language descriptions.
  • Making it affordable to ensure accessibility for those who need it most.
  • Exploring additional features, such as real-time navigation assistance.
